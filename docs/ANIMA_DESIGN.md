# ANIMA Design Document

## Vision

Create a digital world where intelligence emerges naturally.

The project does not script behavior.

Instead, it creates a world with rules.

Intelligence must emerge from interaction.

---

## Philosophy

Reality before gameplay.

Emergence over scripting.

Every action has consequences.

Knowledge must be earned.

Death is part of life.

Learning never stops.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Starting with primitive humans living in forests.

Eventually reaching:

- Villages
- Cities
- Kingdoms
- Civilizations

without hardcoded progression.

---

## Core Pillars

Artificial Life

Emergent Intelligence

Evolution

Survival

Society

Civilization
